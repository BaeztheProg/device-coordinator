Course Syllabus

Computer Science Essentials

Welcome to Computer Science Essentials! This introductory course will expose you to a diverse set of computational thinking concepts and tools. You'll learn visual, block-based programming before transitioning to text-based languages like Python to create apps and develop websites.

Academic Honesty

Honest behavior is expected. Violations may include:

  • Cheating on tests or quizzes.
  • Plagiarism, including misuse of published or electronic materials.
  • Unauthorized collaboration with others.
  • Falsification of data or citations.
  • Unauthorized use of Generative AI.

The consequences for violations can include loss of credit for the assignment, loss of Good Standing privileges, and referral to the administration.
